Radhakrishna Multispeciality Hospital was inaugurated on 23/8/2003 by Padmabhushana Sri Veerendra Heggade Dharmadhikari of Srikshetra Dharmasthala. Their holy grace Sri Sri Balagangadharnatha Swamiji and Sri Sri Keshavananda Bharathi Swamiji blessed the occasion with hearty benedictions to Sri B. Krishna Bhat, Girinagar Founder Father of the Hospital and his son Dr B. K. Vishwanath Bhat- the managing director accompanied by his wife Dr Vidya V. Bhat Medical director.

Radhakrishna Multispeciality Hospital and IVF Centre have always attempted to give the best medical and surgical services in gynaecology, female fertility, and related queries and complications. Since its inception, the hospital strived hard to make a dent in all specialities of medical care aspiring for the dizzy heights of success. Now it can be claimed as a Hi-Tech Super Speciality 50 bedded Hospital outshining in Laparoscopic surgery and other Super Specialities. It is equipped with the latest sophisticated equipment. Our specialised doctors are dedicated to providing you with advanced treatment within a highly professional and friendly environment.
