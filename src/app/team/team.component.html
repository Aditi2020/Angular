DR VIDYA V BHAT

Gynaecology, Obstetrics, IVF

Dr Vidya V Bhat is a well-known Gynaecology, Obstetrics and IVF specialist of Karnataka. She is consulting at Radhakrishna Multispeciality Hospital and IVF Centre and has work experience of 13 years in the profession. She is recognised as one of the authorities in the field of laparoscopic hysterectomy. She is an expert in diagnosing and treating the patients with ease and passionate about her profession. A fervent learner of new techniques and a go-getter, Dr Vidya Bhat specialises in IVF (in vitro fertilisation). She is a specialist in providing services like Laparoscopy, hysterectomy, urogynaecology, and many more. Her versatility as a complete healthcare professional is evident in her role as the Medical Director of Radhakrishna Multispeciality Hospital and in heading the IVF centre there.

Education

1. MBBS- Dr BR Ambedkar Medical College in 1992.
2. MD in Obstetrics and Gynaecology- Kempegowda Institute of Medical Sciences, Bengaluru.
3. DNB (Obstetrics and Gynaecology) title to her qualifications, from the National Board of Examinations, New Delhi.

Experience

1. Her career, spanning over 13 years, started off at Lalbagh Nursing Home where she was Consultant Obstetrician and Gynaecologist.
2. She has extended her expertise to academic studies, as Assistant Professor, Dept of OBG, Rajarajeshwari Medical College, Bengaluru.
3. Medical Director at Radhakrishna Hospital.
4. Course Director for training courses in advanced FOGSI and ICOG recognised endoscopy training and FOGSI IVF training at Radhakrishna Multispeciality Hospital & IVF Centre since 2013.

Achievements

1. Over the years she has presented more than 30 papers and poster presentations on various subjects in both national and international conferences.
2. Some of the topics were: gastroschisis, loop excision for chronic cervicitis, hystero enteric fistula, conjoined twins, mother and fetus.
3. She was awarded the best paper award for Ovarian Drilling.
4. She was the first in the state to conduct a workshop on laparoscopic and vaginal hysterectomy.

Other Interests

1. Dr Vidya Bhat runs an NGO named Radhakrishna Foundation. Many free camps are conducted under its aegis, and over 100 free surgeries have been organised for underprivileged women.
2. She has established the Nulife clinic for women above 45 years and regularly conducts free camps at her centre.
