Bhat Medical director.Radhakrishna Multispeciality Hospital and IVF Centre have always attempted to give the best medical and surgical services in gynaecology, female fertility, and related queries and complications. Since its inception, the hospital strived hard to make a dent in all specialities of medical care aspiring for the dizzy heights of success.Now it can be claimed as a Hi-Tech Super Speciality 50 bedded Hospital outshining in Laparoscopic surgery and other Super Specialities.

It is equipped with the latest sophisticated equipment.Our specialised doctors are dedicated to providing you with advanced treatment within a highly professional and friendly environment.We have done more than 1000 Total Lap Hysterectomies with skills which have been demonstrated in workshops attended by eminent Gynaecologists and Surgeons from all over the state. We are one of the highest volume centres in Karnataka for Total Lap Hysterectomies. Apart from Laparoscopic Hysterectomies, we are also pioneers in doing Lap Myomectomies. We are equipped with the Morcellator to remove the Myoma or Fibroid in parts. Except for ovarian Malignancies which is a contraindication for Laparoscopy, all the other Gynaec Surgeries are efficiently tackled Laparoscopically in this hospital. We also have facilities for Hysteroscopic procedure (Diagnostic & therapeutic) like TCRE (TransCervical Resection of Endometrium) Polupectomies, Myomectomies and office Hysteroscopy. We specialise in both Open & Laparoscopic GI Surgery Procedure.
